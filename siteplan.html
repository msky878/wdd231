Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Moravian-Silesian Tourist Advisor

This name represents a portal that is focused on providing tips and important information to tourists in the Moravian-Silesian region.

Site Purpose:

The site provides a hub where tourists can get tips and information about places of interest in the region. Also, locals will be able to suggest new places, via a form. It will also provide information like weather and maps.

Scenarios:

  • What are some places to visit in this region?
  • What should I be aware of when visiting the region?
  • What are some local customs and traditions?
  • How can I get more people to know about some place?
